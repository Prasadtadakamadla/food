One-Week Indian-Style Diet Plan

Day 1 (Monday)

Breakfast: Vegetable Upma with Coconut Chutney

Mid-Morning Snack: Papaya slices and a handful of roasted nuts (like almonds or walnuts)

Lunch: Rice with toor dal, Bhindi (okra) stir-fry, Small bowl of curd

Afternoon Snack: Fresh coconut water or buttermilk

Dinner: Whole-wheat rotis, Mixed vegetable curry (carrot, beans, peas), Palak paneer (spinach and paneer)

Day 2 (Tuesday)

Breakfast: Moong Dal Chilla with Mint Chutney

Mid-Morning Snack: Apple with a few roasted peanuts

Lunch: Quinoa pulao with veggies like capsicum, peas, and carrots, Sambar with spinach and other seasonal vegetables

Afternoon Snack: Roasted chickpeas with spices

Dinner: Brown rice, Chana masala (chickpea curry), Cucumber-tomato salad

Day 3 (Wednesday)

Breakfast: Idli with Sambar and Tomato Chutney

Mid-Morning Snack: An orange and a few almonds

Lunch: (At office for you) Chicken biryani or chicken curry with rice and curd; For Parents: Vegetable pulao with a small bowl of dal and cucumber raita

Afternoon Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus

Dinner: Whole-wheat rotis, Aloo gobi (potato and cauliflower) curry, Mixed greens stir-fried with garlic

Day 4 (Thursday)

Breakfast: Masala Paratha with Curd

Mid-Morning Snack: A pear and a small handful of seeds (like sunflower or chia)

Lunch: Lemon rice with a side of dal, Cabbage and carrot stir-fry, Small bowl of curd

Afternoon Snack: Roasted peanuts with sliced pineapple

Dinner: Vegetable khichdi with a side of sautéed spinach, Cucumber-tomato-onion salad

Day 5 (Friday)

Breakfast: Besan (gram flour) Chilla with Mint Chutney

Mid-Morning Snack: Seasonal citrus fruit with a few pistachios

Lunch: (At office for you) Chicken biryani or bagara rice with chicken curry and curd; For Parents: Brown rice with rajma (kidney beans), cucumber raita, and a salad

Afternoon Snack: Mixed nuts (almonds, cashews) with a small banana

Dinner: Whole-wheat rotis, Baingan bharta (roasted mashed eggplant), Stir-fried green beans with garlic

Day 6 (Saturday)

Breakfast: Poha with Vegetables and a sprinkle of roasted peanuts

Mid-Morning Snack: A small bowl of berries or seasonal fruits

Lunch: Tomato rice with a side of toor dal, Okra stir-fry (bhindi masala), Small bowl of curd

Afternoon Snack: Greek yogurt with pomegranate seeds

Dinner: Brown rice, Lauki (bottle gourd) and chana dal curry, Light sautéed greens (like spinach or kale)

Day 7 (Sunday)

Breakfast: Whole-grain toast with Scrambled Eggs (for you) or Paneer Bhurji (for parents), with a glass of fresh fruit juice

Mid-Morning Snack: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and a spoon of almond butter

Lunch: Vegetable biryani with a side of raita, A cup of mixed vegetable soup

Afternoon Snack: Mixed fruit bowl with pomegranate, papaya, and kiwi

Dinner: Millet-based roti or whole-wheat roti, Rajma curry (kidney beans), Stir-fried mixed vegetables (carrot, zucchini, capsicum)
